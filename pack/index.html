Installation

ItsDan1el's Pack

  1. Download the pack according to your Minecraft Version
  2. Place the pack in to the resourcepack folder
    • Mac: ~/Library/application support/minecraft/resourcepacks
    • Windows: %appdata%/roaming/.minecraft/resourcepacks
  3. Start Minecraft
  4. Choose the pack in Video Settings -> Resourcepacks
  5. Click Done or reload the pack by pressing F3+T
